Modern Office Suite in North Finchley

A modern 7-office set up with long term professional tenants. The office currently has 3 desks, but could comfortably fit 4. The office is on the 1st floor and access is via the stairs.

Located in Winifred Place, London N12. A great central location on the High Road, North Finchley (in the same building as YVA Solicitors and Starbucks). The property is within close walking distance of Woodside Park Underground station, Northern Line. Close to the Arts Depot and the bus interchange.

The multiple shopping centre on the High Road provides many amenities for staff. Good road communications are offered with the North Circular Road A406, M1 and M25 motorways all within close proximity.
